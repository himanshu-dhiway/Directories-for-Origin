## MCA-21 (Ministry of Corporate Affairs)

**Authority:** Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Government of India

### What You Can Find
- Company registration details
- Directors and their DINs (Director Identification Numbers)
- Charges (loans, securities)
- Financial statements
- Beneficial ownership information

### Direct Access

| Resource | URL | Description |
|----------|-----|-------------|
| **Company Master Data Search** | https://www.mca.gov.in/content/mca/global/en/mca/master-data/MDS.html | Search company details by CIN/name |
| **Director Master Data Search** | https://www.mca.gov.in/content/mca/global/en/mca/master-data/director-master-data.html | Search director information by DIN |
| **Incorporated/Closed Companies** | https://www.mca.gov.in/content/mca/global/en/data-and-reports/company-llp-info/incorporated-closed-month.html | Monthly data on new/closed companies |

### Cost
**FREE** - Public search available for basic information

### Update Frequency
Daily

### Why BFSI Uses This
**Corporate due diligence** - Verifies company legitimacy, ownership structure, and financial health. **Fraud prevention** by checking if directors are disqualified or companies are under investigation.

### Key Features
- Search by Company Identification Number (CIN)
- Search by Company Name
- Director search by DIN
- View company charges and liens
- Download annual returns and financial statements
- Track director appointments and resignations

### Use Cases for BFSI
1. **Loan Applications** - Verify company registration before lending
2. **KYC Compliance** - Validate corporate borrowers
3. **Credit Assessment** - Review financial statements
4. **Risk Management** - Check for director disqualifications
5. **Fraud Detection** - Identify shell companies or fake entities
