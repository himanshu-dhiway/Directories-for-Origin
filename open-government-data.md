## Open Government Data (OGD) Platform

**Authority:** National Informatics Centre, Government of India

### What It Provides
- 560,000+ datasets from 7,800+ government organizations
- Company registration data
- Economic indicators
- Demographic statistics
- Industry reports
- Public sector data

### Direct Access

| Resource | URL |
|----------|-----|
| **Main Portal** | https://www.data.gov.in |
| **Search Datasets** | https://www.data.gov.in/catalogs |
| **API Access** | https://www.data.gov.in/ogpl_apis |

### Cost
**FREE** - All datasets are freely available

### Formats Available
- CSV
- JSON
- XML
- XLS/XLSX
- PDF

### Why BFSI Uses This
**Free government data** - Official statistics on economy, demographics, and industry trends. **Corporate intelligence** - Access company registrations and annual reports without paying MCA fees.

### Key Categories
- Agriculture
- Banking & Finance
- Companies & LLPs
- Economic Statistics
- Education
- Employment
- Health
- Infrastructure
- Trade & Commerce

### Popular Datasets for BFSI
1. Company master data
2. Banking statistics
3. Economic indicators
4. State-wise financial data
5. Trade statistics
6. Consumer price index

## Company Master Data

**Source:** Ministry of Corporate Affairs via data.gov.in

### What It Contains
- CIN (Company Identification Number)
- Company name
- Status (Active/Dormant/Closed)
- Authorized capital
- Paid-up capital
- Registration date
- Registrar of Companies (RoC)
- Business activity classification

### Direct Access

| Resource | URL | Format |
|----------|-----|--------|
| **Company Master Data** | https://www.data.gov.in/catalog/company-master-data | CSV/XLS |

### Why BFSI Uses This
**Bulk company verification** - Download entire company database for mass screening. **Historical tracking** - Monitor corporate status changes over time for risk assessment.

### Use Cases
1. **Batch KYC Screening** - Verify multiple companies at once
2. **Portfolio Monitoring** - Track existing clients' company status
3. **Market Research** - Analyze industry trends by incorporation dates
4. **Risk Alerts** - Identify companies that became dormant/closed
5. **Data Analytics** - Build predictive models for company failure

### How to Use
1. Go to https://www.data.gov.in/catalog/company-master-data
2. Download the latest dataset (updated monthly/quarterly)
3. Import into your database
4. Query for specific companies or patterns
5. Update regularly to maintain fresh data

## MoSPI Compendium

**Full Name:** Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation - Compendium of Datasets and Registries

**Authority:** Government of India

### What It Is
A comprehensive 400+ page document listing ALL official government datasets and registries available in India (2024 edition).

### Direct Access

| Resource | URL | Format |
|----------|-----|--------|
| **Full Compendium (PDF)** | https://www.mospi.gov.in/sites/default/files/Compendium_of_Datasets_and_Registries_in_India_2024.pdf | PDF |

### Why BFSI Uses This
**Discovery tool** - Identifies which government databases exist for each sector. **Compliance reference** - Know all available official data sources for regulatory reporting.

### How to Use
1. Download the PDF
2. Check the index for your sector (e.g., "Financial Services")
3. Navigate to that page number
4. Find the exact database name and accessing authority
5. Contact that authority for data access

### What's Inside
- List of all ministries and their datasets
- Registry names and purposes
- Contact information
- Access procedures
- Update frequencies
- Data formats available

## National Crime Records Bureau

**Authority:** Ministry of Home Affairs

### What It Provides
- Crime statistics by state/district
- Missing persons data (aggregated)
- Accident statistics
- Prison statistics
- Annual crime reports

### Direct Access

| Resource | URL |
|----------|-----|
| **NCRB Open Data** | https://data.gov.in/catalogs/?ministry=National+Crime+Records+Bureau+%28NCRB%29 |
| **Official Website** | https://ncrb.gov.in |

### Why BFSI Uses This
**Geographic risk profiling** - Crime rates by district inform branch location decisions. **Insurance underwriting** - Crime statistics factor into premium calculations for property/life insurance.

### Available Datasets
- State-wise crime data
- District-level statistics
- Crime types and trends
- Conviction rates
- Age/gender demographics of offenders

### Use Cases for BFSI
1. **Branch Planning** - Avoid high-crime areas for new branches
2. **Insurance Pricing** - Adjust premiums based on local crime rates
3. **Risk Assessment** - Geographic risk scoring for loans
4. **Security Planning** - ATM and cash van route planning
5. **Market Analysis** - Demographic insights for product design

### Important Notes
⚠️ **Aggregated data only** - No individual criminal records
⚠️ **Statistical purpose** - Not for background checks
⚠️ **Annual updates** - Data released once per year
